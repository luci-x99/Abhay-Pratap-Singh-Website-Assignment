About Us

Three Decades of
Honest Cooking

A kitchen that has never changed its recipe, never raised its bar on what matters — freshness, hygiene, and taste that feels like home.

Started in Lucknow Cantonment in 1989, Sainik Bhojnalaya built its name on one simple idea: good food doesn't need to be complicated or expensive. It needs to be clean, fresh, and made with care. That's what we've done every single day for over 35 years.

Army personnel, families, office-goers and daily visitors have all made us part of their routine. Slow-cooked dal, seasonal sabzi, chapatis rolled and roasted fresh — no shortcuts, no compromises.
